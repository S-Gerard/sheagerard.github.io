CSCI 443/543 - User Interface Design - Spring 2023

Projects

During the Spring 2023 semester, pairs of students were tasked with designing, prototyping, and evaluating an interactive software prototype. Particular focus was given to understanding and employing Universal Principles of Design and to the iterative process of User Centered Design. Each team was asked to summarize, reflect, and communicate key aspects of the design process as they engaged with it, and to highlight critical milestones, insights and deliverables achieved along the way.
